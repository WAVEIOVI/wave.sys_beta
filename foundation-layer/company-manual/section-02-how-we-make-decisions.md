# SECTION 2: HOW WE MAKE DECISIONS

## 2.1 AUTHORITY MATRIX

This matrix defines who can make what decisions at SS+. Clear authority levels ensure efficient operations while maintaining proper controls.

### Strategic Decisions (Managers Only)
**Approvers:** Karim BEN SALEM & Najla BEN SALEM
- Business strategy, new locations, major capital investments.
- Hiring Responsibles/Key Personnel.
- Company policies, legal matters, and banking.
- **Financial:** Contracts > 10k TND, Discounts > 20%, Write-offs > 2k TND.

### Commercial Authorities
- **Sales Responsible (Najla):** Pricing authority up to 25% discount, Order values up to 25,000 TND.
- **Sales Representatives:** Standard quotations up to 15,000 TND, Discounts up to 15%.
- **Board Tier:** Any discount > 35% or strategic projects with significant margin impact.
  - *Must Escalate:* Sales > 15,000 TND (Rep to Responsible), Sales > 25,000 TND (Responsible to Manager).

### Operations Authorities
- **Operations Responsible:** Up to 5,000 TND (Planned equipment); Up to 1,500 TND (Emergency verbal).
- **Team Leaders:** Up to 1,000 TND (Consumables, service parts).
- **Technicians:** Up to 250 TND (Emergency on-site service recovery).

### Financial & Purchase Authorities
- **Purchase Responsible (Najla):** Up to 10,000 TND (Inventory replenishment, strategic stock).
- **Admin & Finance Responsible:** Routine budget controls; Check processing; Payroll validation.
- **Manager (Karim):** Up to 25,000 TND (Major stock, capital equipment).
- **Board of Directors:** Above 25,000 TND (Major strategic investments).

## 2.3 SIGNATURE AUTHORITY

### Contracts & Agreements
- **Full Authority:** Managers.
- **Limited Authority:** Admin (Routine), Ops (Work Orders/Certificates).

### Financial Documents
- **Checks ≤ 5,000 TND:** One Signature (Manager authorized).
- **Checks > 5,000 TND:** **Dual Signature Mandatory** (Karim + Najla, or designated co-signatory).
- **Checks > 10,000 TND**: Board Notification also required.
- **Invoices:** Admin, Sales, or Ops Responsible.

### Customer Documents
- **Quotations:** Sales Reps (Standard), Sales Responsible (Any).
- **Service Reports:** Technicians (Completion), Team Leaders (Quality).

## 2.4 BUSINESS ETHICS & CONDUCT

SS+ is committed to integrity. All employees must adhere to these guidelines.

### Conflicts of Interest
- **Prohibited:** Financial interest in competitors, steering business to family/friends.
- **Required:** Disclose family relationships or outside business activities annually.

### Gifts & Entertainment
- **Allowed:** Nominal items (pens/calendars), occasional business meals.
- **Prohibited:** Cash/Gift Cards, expensive gifts (> 100 TND), or anything expecting influence.

### Professional Conduct
- **Do:** Be honest, deliver commitments, respect confidentiality.
- **Don't:** Badmouth competitors, disclose customer info, accept bribes.

### Reporting Violations
Report unethical behavior to Supervisor or Manager. No retaliation for good-faith reporting.
