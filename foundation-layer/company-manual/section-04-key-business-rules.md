# SECTION 4: KEY BUSINESS RULES

## 4.1 PRICING GUIDELINES
- **Fair Value & Consistency.**
- **Price List:** Updated quarterly by Sales Responsible.
- **Methods:**
  - *Fire Service:* Per unit.
  - *Products:* Cost + 35% markup (std).
  - *MSA:* Discounted 6-month lock.

## 4.2 DISCOUNT AUTHORITY MATRIX
1. **Sales Representatives:**
   - Standard: Up to 10%
   - High-Volume (>15,000 TND): Up to 12.5%
   - Strategic (Management Approved): Up to 15%
2. **Sales Responsible:** 15.1-25%
3. **Manager (Karim):** 26-35%
4. **Board of Directors:** Above 35% (Strategic/Exceptional).

## 4.3 PAYMENT TERMS & CREDIT
- **New Customers:** Cash/COD or 50% Deposit.
- **Established:** Net 30.
- **Contract:** Net 90.
- **Late Penalties:** 1% per month overdue. Service hold at 60 days.

## 4.4 STANDARDIZED TRAVEL FEES
Travel fees ensure recovery of logistics and specialist time costs.
- **Zone 0 (Upto 120 km):** **Free** (Sfax & Tunis metropolitan areas).
- **Zone 1 (121 - 200 km):** **150 TND**.
- **Zone 2 (201 - 300 km):** **250 TND**.
- **Zone 3 (> 300 km):** Custom Quote (Includes per diem/accommodation).
- **Waiver**: Requires Sales Responsible approval (typically for contracts > 10k TND).

## 4.5 WARRANTY & REWORK
- **Product Returns:** 7 days, unused.
- **Defective Products:** Per manufacturer warranty.
- **Service Rework:** Valid issues reported within **7 calendar days** are corrected within **48 business hours** at zero cost.

## 4.6 RISK MANAGEMENT
- **Decline Credit:** If > 60 days late history or bankruptcy.
- **Decline Job:** Unsafe conditions, outside expertise.
- **Red Flags:** Evasiveness, pressure, bribe offers.

- **Distribution:** All Management (Full), Sales (Full), Ops (Relevant Sections).
- **Updates:** Annual review. Emergency updates by Manager.
