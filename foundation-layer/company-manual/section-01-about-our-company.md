## 1.2 WHAT WE DO

Sécurité Services Plus (SS+) is Tunisia's trusted partner for comprehensive fire safety equipment and workplace safety solutions. We serve businesses of all sizes across Tunisia with quality products, professional installation, and reliable maintenance services.

### A. Fire Safety Equipment & Services

#### Fire Extinguisher Services
- **Sales & Installation:**
  - All types: Powder, CO2, Foam, Water, Clean Agent.
  - All sizes: 1kg to 50kg and larger.
  - Professional installation with proper signage and mounting.
  - Certificate of installation provided.
- **Inspection & Maintenance:**
  - Annual inspection programs.
  - Semi-annual inspection programs.
  - Quarterly inspection for high-risk facilities.
  - Complete maintenance and refilling services.
  - Hydrostatic testing (Pressure vessel integrity test).
  - Compliance documentation.

#### Fire Hose Reel Systems
- Sales and professional installation.
- Inspection and maintenance services.
- Pressure testing and certification.
- Hose replacement and repairs.

#### Fire Detection & Alarm Systems
- Smoke detector sales, installation, and testing.
- Fire alarm system installation and maintenance.
- System inspection and certification.
- Emergency repair services.

#### Other Fire Safety Equipment
- Fire blankets (sales and inspection).
- Fire safety cabinets and storage.
- Emergency lighting systems.
- Fire safety accessories and signage.
- Fire-rated doors and equipment.

### B. Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

We supply comprehensive **PPE** (Personal Protective Equipment) solutions for all industries:

- **Head Protection:** Safety helmets, hard hats (various classes), bump caps, and accessories.
- **Eye & Face Protection:** Safety glasses, goggles, face shields, welding helmets, and prescription safety eyewear.
- **Hand Protection:** Work gloves (leather, cotton, synthetic), chemical-resistant, cut-resistant, heat-resistant, and disposable gloves.
- **Respiratory Protection:** Dust masks, half-face/full-face respirators, filter cartridges (gas, vapor, particle), and escape respirators.
- **Hearing Protection:** Earplugs (disposable/reusable), earmuffs, and communication headsets.
- **Fall Protection:** Safety harnesses, lanyards, anchor points, lifelines, and rescue equipment.
- **Foot Protection:** Safety boots/shoes (steel/composite toe), chemical-resistant, and slip-resistant footwear.

### C. Workwear & Uniforms

- **Industrial Workwear:** Coveralls, work shirts, trousers, High-visibility clothing (EN ISO 20471), and specialized protective clothing.
- **Custom Branding Services:** Logo embroidery, screen printing, heat transfer printing, and corporate uniform programs.
- **Industry-Specific Workwear:** Oil & gas (Flame Resistant FR), construction, manufacturing, food processing (hygienic), healthcare, and hospitality.

### D. Safety Signage & Workplace Safety

- **Safety Signs:** Emergency exit (photoluminescent/LED), Warning, Mandatory, Prohibition, and Fire safety signs.
- **Sign Production:** Various materials (PVC, aluminum, Vinyl), all sizes, installation services, and compliance with international standards.

### E. First Aid & Emergency Equipment

- First aid boxes/kits (all sizes) and room equipment.
- Emergency showers and eyewash stations.
- Spill containment kits.
- Emergency stretchers and blankets.
