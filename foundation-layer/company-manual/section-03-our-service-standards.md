# SECTION 3: OUR SERVICE STANDARDS

## 3.1 CUSTOMER SERVICE COMMITMENTS
Every customer deserves:
- **Respect & Responsiveness.**
- **Expertise & Quality.**
- **Transparency & Reliability.**

## 3.2 RESPONSE TIME STANDARDS

| Priority | Definition | Phone Response | On-Site Arrival | Resolution |
|----------|------------|-----------------|-----------------|------------|
| **P1 - Critical** | Safety risk / System down | 2 Hours | 4 Hours | 12 Hours |
| **P2 - Urgent** | Partial failure / Major impact | 4 Hours | 8 Hours | 24 Hours |
| **P3 - Standard** | Routine maintenance / Repair | 8 Hours | 24 Hours | 48 Hours |

> [!NOTE]
> Response times are calculated from ticket logging. On-site arrival depends on safe transit.

## 3.3 TECHNICAL SERVICE STANDARDS

### Fire Extinguisher Services
- **Inspection:** Visual check per code, photo evidence, tagging, report within 24h.
- **CO2 Standards:** Weight check must be within **5%** of rated capacity to pass; refilling accuracy must be **±2%**.
- **Installation:** Code-compliance placement, secure mounting, signage.

### Quality Assurance
- **Process:** Tech self-check → Team Leader review (100% doc, random physical) → Ops Spot-Check (20% monthly).
- **Rework Policy**: Valid quality issues reported within 7 days are corrected within 48 business hours at zero cost.

### On-Site Protocol
- **Technicians MUST:** Be on time, wear uniform/ID, use **PPE**, clean up, communicate clearly.
- **Technicians MUST NOT:** Smoke on site, use foul language, rush work.

## 3.4 QUALITY GUARANTEES

- **Workmanship Warranty:** 6 months on labor and SS+ installed parts.
- **Re-Inspection window:** Free if reported within **7 calendar days** of intervention.
- **Rework Commitment:** Correction within **48 business hours** of valid report.

## 3.5 COMPLAINT HANDLING PROCESS

1.  **Receive & Acknowledge:** Listen, apologize, document, reference # (Immediate).
2.  **Investigate:** Review records, interview staff (24h).
3.  **Resolve:** Determine fault.
    - *SS+ Fault:* Free rework, credit, apology.
    - *Not SS+ Fault:* Explain, educate.
4.  **Follow-Up:** Confirm satisfaction within 1 week.
